Simple Omlette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for a meal. This classic omlette combines beaten egs coocked to perfection, optinally field with your choise of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, coocked meats, herbs.

Instruction

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of watter or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cool the omlette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (optinal): When the eggs begin to set at the edge but are still slightly in the middle, sprinkle your choosen fillings over one half of the omlette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omlette continues to cook, carrefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it into a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
